## FCAJ Community Day June 2026

**Theme:** Cloud, AI & Agentic Applications in Modern Enterprises

### Purpose of the Event

- Share the development trends of Cloud Computing and AI in modern enterprises.
- Introduce Agentic AI and DevOps Agent applications in system operations.
- Provide a practical perspective on career paths in Cloud and AI.
- Introduce Voice AI, AI Agent technology, and enterprise AI solutions.
- Guide how to apply AI to areas such as DevOps, system operations, and HR management.

### Highlights

**1. Cloud, AI, and Career Opportunities**
- Shared a career journey from Developer to Solution Architect.
- Analyzed the growth of Cloud Computing in Vietnam and worldwide.
- Discussed AI's impact on the tech job market.
- Emphasized the importance of continuous learning and gaining real-world experience early.
- AI will not fully replace tech engineers, but it will change how they work.

**2. Agentic AI in System Operations**

Problems with modern systems:
- Systems are becoming more complex as they move to microservices.
- The volume of logs, monitoring, and observability data is growing fast.
- Incident investigation and resolution take longer.
- Infrastructure operating costs keep increasing.

The Agentic Platform solution:
- Automated incident investigation.
- Support for Cloud cost optimization (FinOps).
- Support for security testing.
- Faster incident resolution and higher operations-team productivity.

**3. Voice AI and AI Agent**

Voice AI architecture:
- Speech-to-Speech Architecture.
- Speech-to-Text → LLM → Text-to-Speech.
- Voice Agent applications in the enterprise.

Live demo:
- Building a Voice Assistant on Amazon Bedrock.
- Using a Knowledge Base to provide domain expertise.
- Integrating an AI Agent to answer product questions.

Challenges:
- Vietnamese is a low-resource language for training data.
- Additional datasets and models tailored to the Vietnamese market are needed.

**4. AWS DevOps Agent**

Common problems:
- Logs and traces scattered across many systems.
- Long time to find the root cause of an incident.
- Difficulty consolidating system-wide context.

AWS DevOps Agent provides:
- Context Learning.
- Memory and Skill Learning.
- Integration with CloudWatch, Slack, ServiceNow.
- Support for root cause analysis.
- Suggested remediation and improvement solutions.

Operating flow:
- Trigger an incident.
- Classify and investigate.
- Propose a resolution.
- Propose long-term improvements.

**5. AI in HR Management**

HR's common difficulties:
- Screening CVs takes a lot of time.
- Hard to evaluate candidate quality.
- Hard to retain top talent.
- Increased workload for the current team when the wrong hire is made.

AI solutions:
- Automatically analyze candidate profiles.
- Generate data to support recruitment.
- Provide HR insights.
- Support building a more effective HR strategy.
