## Sharing and Feedback

### General Assessment

**1. Working Environment**

The working environment at Amazon Web Services (AWS) Vietnam is very professional, open, and friendly. Throughout the internship, I always received support from my mentor and team members whenever I faced difficulties in researching and deploying the project. The workspace and working methods made it easy to focus on learning, research, and developing professional skills. If there were room for improvement, I would hope the program could organize more networking sessions or experience-sharing activities between interns and the AWS engineering team to increase learning and networking opportunities.

**2. Mentor / Team Admin Support**

My mentor always guided me thoroughly while I explored AWS services and built the Budget Tracker project. Whenever I faced difficulties with the system architecture, AWS Serverless, or how to use services such as Amazon Cognito, API Gateway, Lambda, and DynamoDB, I always received very useful feedback and direction. What I appreciated most was that my mentor always encouraged me to research the AWS documentation myself and analyze problems on my own before providing an answer, which helped me improve my ability to think and solve problems independently. In addition, the admin team also provided full support with documentation, schedules, and procedures related to the internship program.

**3. Alignment Between the Work and My Major**

The tasks assigned were closely related to the Information Technology major I am studying. I had the opportunity to apply my knowledge of web programming, RESTful API development, and database design, and especially to work directly with AWS cloud computing services. Directly participating in designing the system architecture, building the application, and deploying it on AWS helped me better understand the Cloud Native and Serverless models, while also expanding my knowledge beyond what is taught at school.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**

Through this internship, I learned many new skills and pieces of knowledge, such as building applications with ReactJS, developing ASP.NET Core Web APIs, and using Amazon DynamoDB, Amazon S3, Amazon Cognito, AWS Lambda, Gemini AI, CloudFront, Route 53, CloudWatch, and Amazon SNS. Besides technical skills, I also improved my ability to read English documentation, research technical materials, work in a team, report progress, and present technical solutions according to enterprise standards.

**5. Culture and Team Spirit**

The working environment has a strong culture of cooperation and mutual support. Team members were always ready to share experience, give feedback, and help me whenever I ran into difficulties during the project. This helped me quickly adapt to a professional working environment and gain more experience collaborating with team members to achieve the project's common goals.

**6. Policies and Programs for Interns**

The internship program is organized methodically with a clear roadmap, helping interns gradually approach AWS technologies and services. Being able to take part in guidance sessions, workshops, and technical discussions with mentors was a great opportunity to deepen practical knowledge. In addition, the flexible working schedule made it possible to balance studying and the internship.

### A Few More Questions

**What did you enjoy most during the internship?**

What I enjoyed most was getting to directly participate in developing the Budget Tracker project on the AWS platform, from designing the system architecture, to deploying AWS services, to building the actual application. This was a great opportunity for me to apply what I had learned and experience the software development process in a business environment.

**What do you think the program should improve for future interns?**

I hope the program can organize more in-depth workshops on AWS services, more real-world deployment scenarios, and more experience-sharing sessions from AWS engineers so interns have more opportunities to learn and broaden their professional knowledge.

**Would you recommend this internship to a friend? Why?**

Yes. I would gladly recommend this internship program to friends because of its professional working environment, dedicated mentors, and highly practical content. The program gives interns the chance to directly work with modern cloud computing technologies, especially the AWS ecosystem, while also developing both technical knowledge and workplace skills.

### Suggestions and Wishes

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

I hope the program can increase the frequency of periodic reviews and feedback sessions between mentors and interns, to provide timely guidance and resolve difficulties during the project. In addition, organizing more sharing sessions on the AWS Well-Architected Framework, DevOps, or real-world projects would help interns gain a more comprehensive view of the process of deploying systems on AWS.

**Would you like to continue with this program in the future?**

Yes. If given the opportunity, I would like to continue participating in and growing alongside this program to further deepen my knowledge of cloud computing, AWS architecture, and developing Cloud Native applications in a real-world environment.

### Other Comments (Open Feedback)

My internship at Amazon Web Services (AWS) Vietnam was a very meaningful experience for me. I not only improved my knowledge of programming and AWS services but also learned how to work according to a professional process, research technical documentation, work in a team, and solve problems. I sincerely thank the mentor team, the guiding staff, and the entire internship program for creating the conditions for me to learn, grow, and successfully complete the Budget Tracker project.
